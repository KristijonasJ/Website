Personality

Kaeya calls himself and Diluc "anti-heroes with attitude problems." Diluc was once a confident and passionate young man, dedicated to his work with the Knights of Favonius. But after his father's untimely death, Diluc grew aloof, bitter, and distant. He dislikes wasting time on idle talk and grows impatient easily.

Diluc holds a particular resentment towards the Knights of Favonius, whom he calls "inefficient" and snubs at every opportunity. This bitterness stems from Inspector Eroch's handling of his father's death, and Diluc has not lessened his bitterness even after Eroch was discharged from the Knights as a traitor.

Appearance

Diluc has porcelain skin with a slim, athletic build, crimson red eyes, and scarlet-hued hair which is always worn in a messy ponytail that extends to his middle back. He dons a black coat that is adorned with golden trimmings and tassels, ending with ebony fur-trims, and wears black pants which are embellished with chains on his right thigh, while his Vision is settled upon his left thigh. Diluc also wears gloves, which are black on the top while the undersides are dyed a shade of red.
